About Vital Lines

Vital Lines is a digital, multilingual magazine that amplifies the work of women journalists reporting on reproductive rights, health, and justice in underreported regions.

Our Mission

We prioritize trauma-informed journalism, fair pay, and publishing in local languages to challenge the colonial frameworks of international reporting.

Our Team

  • Editor-in-Chief — Based in NYC
  • Regional Editors — Remote contributors
  • Freelance Translators and Local Reporters
  • Fact-checkers and Coordinators
